    ABOUT ME

    Discovering music and shedding light on deserving artists through articles or sharing playlists with friends and family has always been one of my favorite pastimes.

    Music decorates time. It allows the fixation on moments and emotions dotting the entire spectrum of good and bad. Listening to all kinds of artists and bands makes me feel less alone in this world, connecting me with both the musicians themselves as well as those around me.

    Saying that, I love (mostly) all music. Listening to it, seeing it live, talking about it, writing about it, discovering it and most importantly having lots and lots of opinions about it.

    I hope to share playlists to accompany your highest highs and lowest lows as well as shed light on deserving artists new and old.
